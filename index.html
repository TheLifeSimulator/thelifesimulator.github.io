Description

LifeSim is an interactive evolutionary sandbox where you watch, or influence, digital organisms evolve over hundreds of generations. Cells compete for resources, reproduce with genetic variation, and adapt to their environment.

You can observe passively or intervene directly. Inspect or modify any cell's genetics, and directly control their behavior. Force mutations. Infect or cure populations. Endless simulations, in an infinite space.

How is it different?

  • Behaviors, interactions, & speciation are all emergent from simple rules, & never the same twice.
  • Immunity, predation, and reproduction compete as evolutionary pressures that spur continuous adaptation.
  • Viruses mutate independently inside hosts, & follow genetic lineages, forcing an evolutionary arms race.

In LifeSim, there are no predetermined outcomes. Watch real emergence arise from genetic variance and viral pressure.
